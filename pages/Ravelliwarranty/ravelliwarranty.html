Ravelli Warranty

  1. Submit this Google form.
  2. Take a picture of the defective item.
  3. Attach that picture with this Destroyed Parts Form Letter and send it to a.trosi@ravelligroup.it, requesting a replacement part be sent.
  4. You will have to "Print" the filled form to a separarte PDF for your changes to take effect.
  5. Pending approval, they will then authorize you to destroy the defective product.
